Part A — Let your food flow in 🔥
So the coach sees your calories + protein. No password, ever.
1
Open the Cronometer app on your phone.
2
Tap the ☰ menu (top-left corner) → tap Settings.
3

4
Find "Export to Apple Health" and switch ON the toggles for Calories and Protein (they turn green).

Part C — Make it run every day 🔁
Set this once so you never have to remember to do it.
1
Open the Shortcuts app → tap "Automation" at the bottom of the screen.
2
Tap the "+" (top-right) → tap "Create Personal Automation."
3
Tap "Time of Day" → pick a time (like 9:00 PM) → make sure it says "Daily" → tap Next.

4
Tap "Run Shortcut," then choose "Send My Steps."
