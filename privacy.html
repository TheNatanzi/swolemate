SwoleMate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SwoleMate is a small private fitness-accountability tool built for a personal group of friends. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Who this is for

SwoleMate is used by a closed group of invited friends only. It is not a public service and does not sell or advertise anything.

What we collect

  • Fitness data you choose to connect: daily steps, workouts, calories, and protein — pulled only from sources you explicitly connect (Cronometer, Oura, or Apple Health via a shortcut you install).
  • Your goals and preferences: the targets and meal times you enter on your setup page.
  • Your WhatsApp number: so the coach can send you and your group the daily summary.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to calculate your daily progress and send a friendly summary message to your private group on WhatsApp. That's it. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Where it lives

Data is stored securely in a private database (Supabase) and is accessible only to the group's organizer. Messages are delivered via the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta).

Your choices

You can stop sharing data at any time by disconnecting the source (Cronometer / Oura / Apple Health) or deleting the shortcut on your phone. To have your stored data deleted, contact the organizer.
